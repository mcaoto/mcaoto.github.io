Who We Are

We are a Christian parent taught academic homeschool cooperative. We meet once a week to teach, grow and build our children up into thriving adults.

What We Do

We have classes available for children newborn to 6th grade. Our subjects include History, Science, Art and Music. We meet from 12:30 to 4. One parent from each family must be an active participant in the co-op in some way.
