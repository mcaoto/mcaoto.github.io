Who We Are

The homeschool community is a rapidly growing community that helps foster discipleship of the next generation. Magnolia Christian Academy provides an environment and opportunity for those families to expand their homeschool experience and foster relationships along the way. Our academic ministry is not only aimed at students but also helps a generation of parents striving to give their children a Christian based education.

Mission Statement

To support homeschooling families by encouraging purposeful relationships, helping build life skills, and providing educational opportunities with a biblical worldview.
