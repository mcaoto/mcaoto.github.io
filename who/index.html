Who We Are

Mission Statement

To support homeschooling families by encouraging purposeful relationships, helping build life skills, and providing educational opportunities with a biblical worldview.

Marilyn Howshall
Why we educate at home
“To provide a specific education track for each of our children in a method and at a pace that is personalized to them. To help us keep the main things the main things!”
“To personalize their education, so we have plenty of family time and are family centered”
“To allow my children to each learn at an individual pace. To give them a strong foundation in Christ and Family”
Importance of being involved in a co-op
“For a resource, to connect with others, and have fellowship. A place for my children to learn to interact with peers and learn to submit to other authority figures (not just parental).”
“To give parents support and encouragement as they continue this path. Allowing my children to learn from other teaching styles, to have healthy competition in their age group and learn to build friendships. Also giving them another person confirming the importance of learning, doing it to the best of their ability and always growing in their knowledge.”
“It provides a trusted environment for our children to learn from others, work on life skills, and develop friendships. It provides me as a mom, encouragement from time spent with like-minded families, and strengthens me in my role.”
