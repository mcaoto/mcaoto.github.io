Who We Are

Purpose

To support homeschooling families by encouraging purposeful relationships, life skills, and providing educational opportunities with a Biblical worldview.

Summary

We are an academic cooperative community of home school parents and students. Our goal is to give homeschool families a safe and fun place to come once a week and learn together. A place where parents use their gifts, skills, and strengths to the teach our children life long skills. We as parents can gain encouragement and support from one another. Our children have the opprutunity to learn from other teaching styles and grow in their ability to learn.

Some of the reasons why we do this

Individual Homeschooling Purpose
To personalize my children's education
Why we educate at home
To provide a specific education track for each of our children in a method and at a pace that is personalized to them. To help us keep the main things the main things!
Why I Homeschool
To personalize their education, so we have plenty of family time and are family centered
Why I Home Educate
To allow my children to each learn at an individual pace. To give them a strong foundation in Christ and Family
Vision for Educating My Children
That my children will be well-educated, kind, successful, spiritual, family oriented, mature, empathetic, always considerate of others, and respectful. That they will have good character, a love for learning, a relationship with Christ, and be different from the rest of the world, all of which will help them excel in life.
My Vision for Home Educating
That my children will be well rounded individuals, finishing with a sound education, a love of learning and character to make the world a better place. That they will have strong relationships with Christ and their family as best of friends and their support team. That they will then be able carry that love and strength over to other relationships. That they will learn to be kind to all, calm in times of stress, strong in times of adversity, knowing when to stand strong and how to be firm with kindness.
Importance of being involved in a co-op
For a resource, to connect with others, and have fellowship. A place for my children to learn to interact with peers and learn to submit to other authority figures (not just parental).
Importance of Being Involved in a Co-op
To give parents support and encouragement as they continue this path. Allowing my children to learn from other teaching styles, to have healthy competition in their age group and learn to build friendships. Also giving them another person confirming the importance of learning, doing it to the best of their ability and always growing in their knowledge.
Why we love co-op
It provides a trusted environment for our children to learn from others, work on life skills, and develop friendships. It provides me as a mom, encouragement from time spent with like-minded families, and strengthens me in my role.
I want my kids to know and understand that it isn't just the knowledge that is important, but the ability to learn and understand something in a way that they can apply it that will take them farther.
Each of my children see things from a different perspective, and those angles expose various aspects of how they understand things. I want them to be able to harness that ability as a strength, but to also balance it with a respect and value for the differences in how other people see things. Then having the encouragement to collectively bring a more complete picture together with others, they can build from a more solid foundation.
I was home schooled myself and the value that my parents added to my education I want to provide to my own children, allow them to relate to people at a deeper level, and realize the potential that exists for each of them.
I value the fact that I was home schooled and taught how to learn. Understanding fundamentally how to learn something has been one of the things that has enabled me to accomplish great things in life, and that is one things I want to pass to my own children.
